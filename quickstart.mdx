# Quick start

This guide will help you get started with DevAurva and understand how to choose the right pricing plan for your web development needs.

## Step 1: Understand the pricing tiers

DevAurva offers four flexible pricing tiers designed to match your business stage and requirements.

### Budget (R2,000 - R10,000)

Perfect for small businesses and startups looking to establish their online presence.

**Key features:**
- Strategic planning and basic design
- Domain registration and setup
- 5 business email accounts
- 3 months premium hosting
- Basic SEO setup
- Mobile-responsive design
- Contact form integration
- Social media links
- Basic analytics setup
- 3 months support and maintenance

<Tip>
  The Budget tier is ideal if you need a professional business website to get started quickly.
</Tip>

### Business (R10,000 - R25,000)

Ideal for growing businesses that need enhanced features and branding.

**Key features:**
- All Budget features included
- Enhanced branding package
- 100 business email accounts
- 6 months premium hosting
- Advanced SEO optimization
- Blog/news section
- Newsletter integration
- Custom forms and features
- Google Business integration
- 3 months support and maintenance

<Note>
  This is our most popular tier, offering the best balance of features and value for growing businesses.
</Note>

### Professional (R25,000+)

For established businesses requiring custom web applications and advanced features.

**Key features:**
- All Business features included
- Custom web application
- E-commerce integration
- Unlimited email accounts
- 12 months premium hosting
- Advanced security features
- Custom API development
- Database integration
- Advanced analytics
- 6 months support and maintenance

### Custom Solution

Build your perfect package with tailored features and flexible pricing.

**Key features:**
- Tailored to your needs
- Pick and choose features
- Custom development
- Flexible pricing
- Scalable solutions
- Priority support

<Info>
  Not sure which tier is right for you? Contact us for a custom quote and we'll help you choose the best solution.
</Info>

## Step 2: Evaluate your requirements

Before choosing a pricing tier, consider the following:

1. **Project scope** - What type of website or application do you need?
2. **Required features** - Do you need e-commerce, custom forms, or API integration?
3. **Timeline** - When do you need your project launched?
4. **Budget** - What is your budget range for this project?
5. **Growth plans** - Do you plan to add more features in the future?

## Step 3: Contact us for a quote

Once you've identified your requirements:

1. Navigate to the contact form on the DevAurva website
2. Provide details about your project
3. Specify which pricing tier interests you (or select Custom)
4. Include any specific requirements or questions
5. Submit your inquiry

Our team will review your request and respond with a detailed quote and timeline.

<Tip>
  Be as specific as possible about your requirements to receive an accurate quote. Include information about desired features, design preferences, and any integrations you need.
</Tip>

## Step 4: Start your project

After accepting the quote:

1. **Strategic planning** - We'll work with you to define project scope and requirements
2. **Design phase** - Our team creates mockups and design concepts for your approval
3. **Development** - We build your website or application using modern technologies
4. **Testing** - Thorough quality assurance and testing before launch
5. **Launch** - Deploy your project and provide training on how to use it
6. **Support** - Ongoing maintenance and support based on your chosen tier

## What's included in every project

Regardless of which tier you choose, all DevAurva projects include:

- **Mobile-responsive design** - Your site works perfectly on all devices
- **Modern technology stack** - Built with React, Express, and MongoDB
- **Security best practices** - Protected against common vulnerabilities
- **SEO foundation** - Optimized for search engines from day one
- **Email notifications** - Automated email systems for forms and user actions
- **Professional support** - Dedicated support during and after development
